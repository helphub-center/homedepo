What is HomeDepot.com/MyCard?
----------------------------
HomeDepot.com/MyCard is the official online portal for Home Depot credit card holders.
It allows users to securely manage their card account, payments, and balances online.

Things You Need Before Login
----------------------------
- Home Depot credit card  
- Registered username and password  
- Internet-connected device  

How to Log In at HomeDepot.com/MyCard
------------------------------------
1. Open a web browser on your device.
2. Visit **HomeDepot.com/MyCard**.
3. Enter your username.
4. Enter your password.
5. Click **Sign In**.
6. Your account dashboard will open.

How to Make a Payment Online
----------------------------
1. Sign in to your account.
2. Select the **Payments** option.
3. Choose your payment method.
4. Enter the payment amount.
5. Submit the payment.

What You Can Do After Login
---------------------------
- View account balance  
- Review recent transactions  
- Make or schedule payments  
- Manage account details  

Common Issues and Solutions
---------------------------
- **Forgot password:** Use the password recovery option.
- **Login failed:** Verify your login details.
- **Page not loading:** Try a different browser or clear cache.

FAQs
----
**Can I access HomeDepot.com/MyCard on mobile?**  
Yes, the website works on mobile and desktop browsers.

**Is registration required?**  
Yes, first-time users must register their card.

**Is HomeDepot.com/MyCard secure?**  
Yes, it uses standard security measures to protect user data.
